accidentsHappen

A story about our teammates car inspired us to use a dataset to analyze and predict the chances of getting in an accident based on specific criteria and data. We see great potential in utilizing this dataset to provide interesting insights and research. By entering certain criteria such as zip codes, seasonal trends, severity, and traffic attributes, one can better forecast the chances of getting into a car accident. It is a simple and easy tool that any driver can use.


The Severity of an accident is a measure of the degree of damage and harm done on objects and people, as well as the affecting the natural flow traffic in the during the accident. The Severity Level, a range of numbers 1 to 4, indicates impact on traffic, such as 1 being short delay as a result of the accident and 4 indicates a significant impact on traffic (i.e., long delay).
